ABOUT US

I am so excited and really quite honoured that you have decided to join us. So first up a big thank you from the bottom of my heart!

So as you may already know my name is Sarah and I started Daisy's Closet a little over a year and a half ago when my youngest daughter was turning 2 with my beautiful mum and best friend Margaret.

As a mother myself of 3 beautiful children it was my desire to create a business where I could work from home ans be a very present part in helping my children to blossom while they are young.

So with the support of my amazing husband I decided to share my passion of women's fashion with the world

Being a busy, on the go mum myself, I know how important it is to be able to purchase gorgeous, high quality and affordable clothing that takes the thought out of that what am I going to wear every day.

So I have created a one stop shop for everyBODY to access high quality everyday, effortless pieces that will take the stress out of your dressing

COMPANY'S VALUES

Daisy' Closet stands for inspiring your inner beauty and expressing yourself in what you choose to wear.

Mission

It's about supporting every woman in the world to bring out her effortless chic style in what she wears and letting her inner confidence shine bright.

Vision

We are a mum and daughter Australian based family business passionate about providinhgs a one step shop formal women to come together and shop affordable, high quality size inclusive offering sizes 6-26 with worldwide shipping.
